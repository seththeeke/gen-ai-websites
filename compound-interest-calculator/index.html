Compound Interest Calculator

Welcome to the Compound Interest Calculator! This tool helps you calculate the growth of your savings or investments over time. You can track multiple accounts and see how your balance grows with different interest rates, contributions, and time periods.

How to use:

  • Click "Add Account" to create a new account entry.
  • Fill in the account details (name, principal, contributions, interest rate, and time period).
  • Click "Calculate" to see how each account will grow.
  • Your data will be saved in your browser only, so you don't need to re-enter it next time. This data is not stored in any database.
